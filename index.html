Level

Check the glasses display for the bubble vial.

How to use

  • Place the glasses on a surface you trust to be level.
  • Tap the temple touchpad to zero the reference — the offset persists across launches.
  • Tap again to clear the reference.
  • Double-tap to exit.

Reading the vial

The bubble (●) rises toward the high side, like a spirit level. When it sits between the inner ticks (within ±0.5°) the header shows ★ LEVEL ★.

Calibration

Calibration is stored on the device via the Even companion.

Uses the glasses' IMU at 10 Hz. Roll only — pitch is not shown.
